Welcome to the Services Page where we explain exactly what it is that we have to offer!

1. Rent-a-Dog

We provide well trained and friendly dogs of various breeds! All our dogs come with a leash and harness which MUST always be worn when taken outdoors. We recommend that you come in and see the dogs prior to renting to ensure that the dog is a perfect fit for you! All dogs come with a bag of treats, food dependant on what times you are renting, dishes and various toys based on the dogs' favourites at the time!

2. Rent-a-Cat

We have cats of all temperments that are suited just for you! All our cats come with a leash and harness which MUST always be worn when taken outdoors. We recommend that you come in and see the cats prior to renting to ensure that the cat is a perfect fit for you! All cats come with a bag of treats, food dependant on what times you are renting, dishes and various toys based on the cats' favourites at the time!

Costs:

You are allowed to rent a dog for $20 per hour for a maximum of 8 hours per day. Each dog can only be rented once per day. On top of the rental fee we also require a $200 deposit to ensure that the dog will be returned to us. When the dog is returned to us in the same condition as when it left, the deposit is refunded with no extra charges. Upon arrival have a piece of photo I.D. ready so we can scan it and keep it on file and also have you $200 deposit ready.
