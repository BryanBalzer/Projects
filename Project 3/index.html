About Us

We are a company that provides a service where you can rent a dog or a cat of your choice. Here at Rent-a-Pet we understand that some peoples lives are too busy to be able to care for a pet of their own. Therefore, this service allows you to temporarily love a pet of your choice. Pets are amazing companions and help to improve mental health. We are a new company so we only have a select amount of candidates to be chosen from. However, we are always looking to expand our roster!
